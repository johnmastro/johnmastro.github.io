Blog reboot

2018-02-13

I’ve had a site here since 2013 but, for a variety of reasons, I haven’t posted anything since early 20141. I want to start writing again now, and this time I have a better idea what I want to write about, at least to start.

I also want to ditch the old stuff and start fresh. I know, it’s bad form to break URLs, but in this case the damage is nominal. Either way, I’ve already decided to go for the reboot, and in fact have already done it. (However, I did preserve the Atom feed’s location, so neither of my subscribers will need to change anything.)

With that out of the way, I’ll be back soon with a new post.
